Radha Delamarter is currently Married to her fourth husband; Divorce and Children

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, June 23, 2024

Radha Delamarter is a prominent American actress best known for her appearance in the movies such as Lucky Luke (1992) and Who Shot Jhonny Rock? (1991). 

Moreover, she is also popular as the ex-wife of the American actor Timothy Busfield. Apart from Timothy, she also has three other marital relationships.

Delamarter's Current Relationship Status

Radha Delamarter is currently in a married relationship with her long-term husband, Frederick David Sperberg. The duo exchanged vows with each other on November 23, 1990.

Following their marriage, the romantic duo has led a happy married life. They together share a child whose name has not been shared in public.

Was Married Four Times

As mentioned earlier, Radha Delamarter has been married four times and has divorced three of her husbands.

The American actress tied the knot with her first husband, Stephen K Longley, on 18 March 1973. The couple remained in a marital relationship for 7 years and divorced. They have one child together.

Second Marriage with Stephen 

Delamarter then dated Stephen Quentin Cowie and celebrated her wedding with her second husband on December 21, 1978. 

The matrimonial relationship between Delamarter and Stephen did not last long. The former duo divorced after having a child. However, the reason behind their separation is still unknown.

Third Wedding

Delamarter tied the knot for the third time with the American actor and director Timothy Busfield in 1981. The former partners together share a son named Wilson Busfield, born in 1982. 

Everything was going well between the married couple until 1986. The former couple started having many ups and downs in their relationship, which led them to walk their separate ways. They ended their five years of the marital relationship and divorced each other by the end of 1986.

Third Husband: Timothy Busfield

Her ex-husband, Timothy Busfield, is a famous actor and director. To date, he has appeared in over 20+ movies and TV series. Likewise, he is well-known for his role as Elliot Western on Thirtysomething and as Mark on Field of Dreams.

During his career, he has also worked with some other popular stars like Meghan Ory and Suzanne Somers. Busfield is currently married to his third wife, Melissa Gilbert.

Net Worth & Salary

Being in the entertainment industry for a very short time, Radha Delamarter might not have earned much to live a luxurious life. Although she is not currently involved in the entertainment industry, she might be involved in another profession to support her living.

Having in some movies in the 1990s, she probably earned around $100,000. Her net worth is estimated to be around $1 million. Moreover, the three-time divorcee, Radha, might have received some cash during the divorce settlement and child support.

On the other hand, her ex-husband, Timothy Busfield, has a whopping net worth of $3 million. He has secured this huge sum of money through his acting career.

Early Life: Her Age & Bio 

The prominent actress Radha was born in the USA as Deborah Sue Delamarter. Moreover, she holds an American nationality and belongs to the white ethnicity. 

She grew up in her hometown under the guidance of her parents. However, she has not revealed many details regarding her early life in any media.

Radha Acting Journey

Radha initially came into the limelight after she appeared in the 1991 movie, Who Shot Johnny Rock? as well as Lucky Luke in 1992. She initially started her acting journey in 1985 by portraying the role of Woman in Restaurant in the Family Ties TV series.

Following it, she appeared in a short movie titled Summer's End portraying the role of Mildred Buckwalter. Altogether, she has appeared in twelve movies and TV series throughout her acting journey from 1985 to 1994.

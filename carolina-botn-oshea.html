Carolina Botn O'Shea- Meet Daughter of Emilio Botn and Paloma O'Shea

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, April 15, 2024

Carolina Botín O'Shea is known as the daughter of late Spanish banker, Emilio Botín and his wife, Paloma O'Shea, a pianist. Emilio was known for being the executive chairman of a Spanish multinational financial services company named Grupo Santander.

Unfortunately, Emilio passed away of a heart attack on 9th September 2015 at age 87 in Madrid. Today we are exploring more details about Carolina including her bio, boyfriend, and others. 

Does Carolina Botín O'Shea Have a Husband?

The celebrity kid, Carolina Botín O'Shea has always lived a low key life since her early days. So most of her personal information is not available on the internet. When it comes to her personal life, she is a married woman. 

As of now, Carolina is in a happy marital relationship with her husband, Christian Shina, a Korean-born German physician. They tied the knot in 1989 in a private ceremony on the Puente San Miguel estate. Ever since the couple has been enjoying each other company for more than 31 years.

On the other hand, her sister, Carmen Botín was married to her ex-husband, Severiano Ballesteros Sota aka Seve Ballesteros from 1988 to 2004. He is a Spanish retired World No. 1 golfer.

Together, they had three children, two sons Javier Ballesteros (1991), Migue Ballesteros (1993), and daughter Carmer Ballesteros (1995). Also, she has three nephews named Ricardo Gomez-Acebo, Carla Gomez-Acebo, and Jose Maria Gomez-Acebo, from her sister' Paloma's side.

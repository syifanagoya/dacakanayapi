Rico Swavey (Ex-BBNaija Housemate) Cause of Death

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, May 26, 2024

Rico Swavey became a reality TV star and ex-BBnaija housemate by default. Moreover, he ventured into the Nigerian movie industry, known as Nollywood. Known for his role in the “Life 101” drama series, Rico, two years ago released a song titled” Afrosantana.” He had a good music and movie career in his lifetime.

Rico Swavey Cause Of Death

Former housemate of Big Brother Naija season 3, Patrick Fakoya, aka Rico Swavey has succumbed to the cold hands of death following his involvement in a car crash

His management had on Wednesday announced that he was placed on life support as his situation was critical and doctors were battling to save his life, however, his colleagues Tobi Bakre and Alex Unusual stated that Rico was dead.

They shared a picture of him and wrote, ”This was not what we agreed Rico. Rest In Peace #ricoswavey. ” Alex said on Twitter. Rico’s family and management are yet to confirm the development.

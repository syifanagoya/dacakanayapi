Early Life & Biography

Joakim Simon Noah was born on 25th February 1985, in New York City to singer and tennis player Yannick Noah and four-time runner-up and Miss Sweden Cecilia Rodhe. Joakim Noah has three siblings. His grandfather was also a famous Cameroonian professional football player.

In 1988 he shifted to Paris, France, and lived there till 1998. After that, he returned to New York City at the age of 13.

He started playing from college basketball and later on picked by Chicago Bulls in the NBA. Noah has a French father and a Swedish mother. Hence he has multiple citizenships of America, France, and Sweden.

Personal Life

Joakim Noah started dating Lais Ribeiro, who is Victoria’s Secret model. The following year, they got engaged in the presence of family and friends. He has been able to keep his personal life away from the limelight and is completely focused on his career.

In 2010, along with his mother, he founded the Noah’s Arc Foundation to help children for positive self-expression through art and sports. The foundation mainly focuses on the underprivileged and at-risk youth in Chicago.

Age, Height, and Weight

Being born on 25 February 1985, Joakim Noah is 39 years old as of today’s date 20th July 2024. His height is 2.11 m tall, and his weight is 105 kg.

Career

Noah started his basketball career by playing in college basketball for the Florida Gators and won many NCAA championships in 2006 and 2007. After 2007, he was picked by the Chicago Bulls as the ninth overall pick in the 2007 NBA draft.

In the 2009-10 session of the NBA, he took Chicago Bulls to the playoffs, but they lost to the Cleveland Cavaliers in the first round in five games.

In 2010-11 Noah signed a five-year contract extension with the bulls for $60 million. On 15th December 2010, he had ligament damage in his hand. He returned on 23 February 2011.

On 22nd February 2012, he made his first career triple-double against Milwaukee Bucks. It was memorable for the bulls because, after Artis Gilmore, he was the first player to do so.

In the 2012-13 session, he recorded his career-best of 30 points and 23 rebounds in a winning game against the Detroit Pistons. In the same session, he recorded his second-ever triple-double in a victory game over the Boston Celtics.

On 2nd March 2014, in a game against New York Knicks, he recorded his fifth career triple-double.

On 21st April 2014 for the first time in his career, he was awarded the NBA Defensive Player of the Year award.  In the same year, he was named to the 2014 All-NBA first team.

Later in 2016, Noah played for the hometown team New York Knicks, but in 2018 he had a dispute with the team as they wanted to release him from the team.

On 4 December 2018, Noah was signed by the Memphis Grizzlies, and he played with them till 2019. On 9th March 2020, the Los Angeles Clippers signed a 10-day contract with Noah.

Awards & Achievements

Noah was awarded many awards in his illustrious career. In 2014, he won the NBA Defensive Player of the Year Award. The following year, he was awarded the J. Walter Kennedy Citizenship Award.

He was included in the 2014 All-NBA First Team, 2013-14 NBA All-Defensive first team, 2013-14 NBA All-Star, 2011 NBA All-Defensive Second Team, and many more.

Noah was part of the team that won the NCAA Championship in 2006-2007 and was named NCAA final four most outstanding players in 2006.
